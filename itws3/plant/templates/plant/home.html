Our Project Work

Here is the work of our team members on plant monitoring system.


Our project mainly focuses on welfare of the farmer . In this project we look after the plant statistics such as temperature , humidity , soil moisture , water level and we take these values. with the soil moisture value we predict the plant condition whether it needs irrigation or not and e include special comnditions such as rainfall and drought. In case of rainfall the irrigation is stopped and in case of drought we supply the water . we take care of the location of the plant and depict the condition of plant by changing its label color and its surroundings water level is also shown in the map of the water body whether it is completely filled or nill based on its level by showing an image in the map We also provide the login advantage for security purposes and adding more plants to the project probiding an easy way for a farmer for his agriculture.
